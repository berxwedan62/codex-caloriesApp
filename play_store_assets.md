Calories Tracker helps you build consistent nutrition habits without complexity.

- Log meals and calorie totals in seconds.
- Keep your data on-device for a privacy-first experience.
- Review your daily entries at a glance.
- Stay focused with a clean, lightweight interface.
- Start quickly with no account required.

This app is designed for simple daily tracking during early internal testing. As features expand, we will keep the experience fast, understandable, and respectful of your privacy.
